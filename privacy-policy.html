StudyFlow Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: September 20, 2025
Quick Summary: StudyFlow stores your data locally on your device, doesn't sell your information, and only collects what's necessary to provide you with the best study experience.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Email address (for account creation and support)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • Device information (for app functionality and troubleshooting)

1.2 Usage Data

  • Assignment and task data (stored locally on your device)
  • App usage statistics (anonymous)
  • Crash reports and error logs (anonymous)

1.3 Local Storage

  • All your assignments, tasks, and study data are stored locally on your device
  • We do not upload your personal study content to our servers

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Provide assignment tracking and task management features
  • Sync data across your devices (if you enable cloud sync)
  • Send notifications for assignments and deadlines

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance
  • Fix bugs and add new features
  • Provide customer support

3. Information Sharing

We Do Not Sell Your Data: We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your study data remains private and secure.

3.1 Third-Party Services

  • Canvas API: We connect to your school's Canvas system only with your explicit permission
  • Analytics: We use anonymous analytics to improve the app
  • Payment Processing: Secure payment processing for premium features

4. Data Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • Your data is primarily stored on your device
  • We use encryption for any data transmission

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard security practices
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Secure authentication for account access

5. Your Rights

5.1 Data Access & Control

  • View all data we have about you
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of analytics and marketing communications
  • Export your assignments and tasks

6. Children's Privacy

  • StudyFlow is designed for users 13 years and older
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • Parents can contact us to review or delete their child's data

7. Canvas Integration

  • We only access Canvas data you explicitly authorize
  • Canvas credentials are securely stored and encrypted
  • You can revoke Canvas access at any time

8. International Users & Compliance

  • We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws
  • Data may be processed in countries other than your own with appropriate safeguards

9. Updates to This Policy

  • We may update this privacy policy from time to time
  • You will be notified of significant changes through the app
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of updates

10. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

Email: privacy@studyflow.app

Support: support@studyflow.app

Response Time: We aim to respond to privacy requests within 30 days.

By using StudyFlow, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy policy.
