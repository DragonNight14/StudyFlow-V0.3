STUDYFLOW PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: September 20, 2025

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Information
- Email address (for account creation and support)
- Name (optional, for personalization)
- Device information (for app functionality and troubleshooting)

1.2 Usage Data
- Assignment and task data (stored locally on your device)
- App usage statistics (anonymous)
- Crash reports and error logs (anonymous)

1.3 Local Storage
- All your assignments, tasks, and study data are stored locally on your device
- We do not upload your personal study content to our servers

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

2.1 Core Functionality
- Provide assignment tracking and task management features
- Sync data across your devices (if you enable cloud sync)
- Send notifications for assignments and deadlines

2.2 App Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance
- Fix bugs and add new features
- Provide customer support

2.3 Communications
- Send important app updates and announcements
- Respond to your support requests
- Notify you about new features (with your consent)

3. INFORMATION SHARING

3.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data
- We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information
- Your study data remains private and secure

3.2 Third-Party Services
- Canvas API: We connect to your school's Canvas system only with your explicit permission
- Analytics: We use anonymous analytics to improve the app
- Payment Processing: Secure payment processing for premium features

3.3 Legal Requirements
- We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

4. DATA SECURITY

4.1 Local Storage
- Your data is primarily stored on your device
- We use encryption for any data transmission

4.2 Security Measures
- Industry-standard security practices
- Regular security updates and patches
- Secure authentication for account access

5. YOUR RIGHTS

5.1 Data Access
- View all data we have about you
- Request a copy of your data

5.2 Data Control
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt-out of analytics and marketing communications
- Control what data is shared with third-party services

5.3 Data Portability
- Export your assignments and tasks
- Transfer data between devices

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

6.1 Age Requirements
- StudyFlow is designed for users 13 years and older
- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
- If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately

6.2 Parental Controls
- Parents can contact us to review or delete their child's data
- We encourage parental involvement in their child's app usage

7. COOKIES AND TRACKING

7.1 Local Storage
- We use local storage to save your preferences and settings
- No tracking cookies are used for advertising purposes

7.2 Analytics
- Anonymous usage analytics to improve app performance
- You can opt-out of analytics in the app settings

8. INTERNATIONAL USERS

8.1 Data Transfer
- Your data may be processed in countries other than your own
- We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place

8.2 Regional Compliance
- We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws
- Contact us for region-specific privacy rights

9. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

9.1 Policy Changes
- We may update this privacy policy from time to time
- You will be notified of significant changes
- Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of updates

9.2 Notification
- In-app notifications for major policy changes
- Email notifications (if you've provided an email address)

11. SPECIFIC FEATURES

11.1 Canvas Integration
- We only access Canvas data you explicitly authorize
- Canvas credentials are securely stored and encrypted
- You can revoke Canvas access at any time

11.2 Pomodoro Timer
- Timer data is stored locally on your device
- No personal timing data is transmitted to our servers

11.3 Assignment Tracking
- All assignment data remains on your device

11.4 Notifications
- Push notifications for assignment deadlines
- You can disable notifications in device settings

12.1 Account Data
- We retain account data as long as your account is active

12.2 Anonymous Data

14. CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
- Delete personal information we have collected
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

15. EUROPEAN PRIVACY RIGHTS (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate personal data
- Erase your personal data
- Restrict processing of your personal data
- Data portability
- Object to processing
